About Me

I'm a second-year Design major with a minor in Psychology studying at Flame University. My design journey has been shaped by hands-on experiences that combine creativity, technology, and impact.

In the Royal Enfield x Maker's Asylum Ghost Net Challenge, I explored using AI and sustainable materials to help remove ghost nets. At the CIID Workshop, I worked with immersive technology, designing an AI-powered sofa that interacts with users.

Through my internship with Teach for India, I developed an empathy-driven approach to design by working closely with children. These experiences have helped me develop a well-rounded approach to design, focusing on accessibility, sustainability, and innovation.
